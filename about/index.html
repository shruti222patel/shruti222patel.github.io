Hugo is a static site engine written in Go.

It makes use of a variety of open source projects including:

  • Cobra
  • Viper
  • J Walter Weatherman
  • Cast

Learn more and contribute on GitHub.

Setup

Some fun facts about Hugo:

  • Built in Go
  • Loosely inspired by Jekyll
  • Primarily developed by spf13 on the train while commuting to and from Manhattan.
  • Coded in Vim using spf13-vim

Have questions or suggestions? Feel free to open an issue on GitHub or ask me on Twitter.

About

Since my early days I've always questioned: 'What is the purpose of life?' The search for that answer has lead me down different paths -- family, religion, knowledge, and apathy. I still have no freaking idea. So, I'm ripping away all my preconceived notions and building up my mind, body, and life from the basics and my love of coding. One thought, one morsel, one app at a time.
